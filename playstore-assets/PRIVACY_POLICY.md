# Privacy Policy — Iron Fury: Last Stand

**Last updated:** March 17, 2026

## Overview

Iron Fury: Last Stand ("the Game") is a single-player offline action game. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

The Game does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Specifically:

- No personal information is collected (name, email, phone number, etc.)
- No location data is accessed
- No analytics or tracking services are used
- No advertisements are displayed
- No third-party SDKs that collect data are included
- No internet connection is required to play

## Local Storage

The Game stores the following data **locally on your device only**, using Android SharedPreferences:

- High score
- Coin balance
- Unlocked weapons
- Control size preference

This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the Game.

## Children's Privacy

The Game does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will post the new version here with an updated date.
