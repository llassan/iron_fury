Iron Fury: Last Stand is an action-packed side-scrolling shooter inspired by the classic run-and-gun games of the arcade era. Battle through 20 uniquely themed levels, face 20 fearsome boss battles, unlock a roster of playable heroes, and master 5 powerful weapons in your fight for survival.

⚔️ FEATURES

★ 20 Unique Levels — From lush forests, scorching deserts, frozen peaks, volcanic depths and neon cities to sunken ruins, sky fortresses, haunted swamps, crystal caverns, toxic wastelands, abyssal trenches, molten cores, aurora tundras, orbital stations, blood marshes, golden dunes, void nebulas, ashen battlefields and the dragon's throne. Each level features a distinct visual theme, platform layout, and escalating difficulty.

★ 20 Boss Battles — Every level ends with a boss showdown. Face the War Machine, Sand Scorpion, Ice Titan, Magma Lord, Cyber Overlord, Kraken, Storm Emperor, Swamp Horror, Crystal Guardian, Supreme Commander — and tougher foes beyond, including the Plague Warden, Leviathan, Inferno Colossus, AI Sentinel, Star Devourer and the Omega Destroyer. Each boss has distinct attack patterns that evolve as you deal damage.

★ Unlockable Characters — Choose your hero! Start as the Recruit and unlock new characters like the Commando, Arctic Ops, Night Owl, Desert Fox and Crimson Elite using coins you earn in battle. Each has its own look.

★ 5 Powerful Weapons — Start with the Machine Gun and unlock the Spread Gun, Laser, Rocket Launcher, and Flamethrower. Buy weapons permanently with coins, or grab power-up drops from defeated enemies for an instant weapon switch.

★ Power-Up System — Defeated enemies may drop weapon power-ups. Grab them to instantly switch to a new weapon mid-battle. Stay sharp and adapt your strategy on the fly!

★ Prone Stance & Tactical Moves — Drop prone to take cover and lay down fire, double-jump across deadly gaps, and read incoming enemy fire at a glance — enemy bullets glow red.

★ Adjustable Controls — Customize your on-screen D-pad and button sizes (S/M/L/XL) to match your playstyle. Your preference is saved automatically.

★ Procedural Audio — Retro-style sound effects and music generated in real-time. No downloads needed, tiny app size.

★ Beautiful Parallax Backgrounds — Multi-layered scrolling with mountains, trees, clouds, and dynamic skies create immersive environments that change with every level.

★ Offline Play — No internet required. Play anywhere, anytime.

★ Tiny Size — No bloat, just pure action.

🎮 CONTROLS
• D-Pad: Move and aim
• Jump button: Leap over obstacles and enemies
• Fire button: Unleash your weapon
• Tap weapon selector to switch between unlocked weapons

💀 CAN YOU BEAT ALL 20 LEVELS?
The difficulty ramps up with every level — more enemies, trickier platforms, and increasingly powerful bosses. Only the most skilled players will survive to the very end and defeat the Omega Destroyer.
